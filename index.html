🏫 About Nepal Police School (NPS)

Nepal Police School (often called Police School or NPS) is a prestigious co‑educational residential school established in 2040 B.S. (1983 CE) under the welfare program of the Nepal Police Organization. The central school in Sanga, Kavre was founded by the late Inspector General of Police D.B. Lama to educate children of police personnel and later opened to civilian students as well.

Located about 21 km east of Kathmandu on a hillside campus of over 200 acres of forests and orchards, NPS offers a disciplined, healthy learning environment. It admits students from Grade 4 to Grade 12, following the NEB curriculum, preparing learners for SEE and SLC examinations.

The school’s motto is: “Better Schooling for Ideal Citizens.”

🎯 Vision & Mission

🔹 Vision: To transform Nepal Police School into an ideal learning center that molds students into disciplined, competent, and socially responsible citizens.

🔹 Mission: To foster excellence, creativity, compassion, leadership, and ecological concern in students. The mission emphasizes forming conscientious learners who can face challenges with confidence and contribute positively to society.

🎓 Admissions Information

Admissions are generally open for Classes IV–VIII and XI (Science and Management streams). New sessions typically begin after SEE results with notices issued in Fagun.

Entrance Exam Details:

Written exams for English, Nepali, Mathematics and Science (Grades IV–VIII).

Science stream students prepare for MCQs in higher‑level subjects; Management stream for combined language and math.

Admission decisions are based on entrance exam results and interviews. This structured process ensures quality and merit‑based selection.

📚 Facilities & Campus Life

🏡 Residential Life: Hostel living is mandatory for most grades. A supportive team — including wardens and staff — ensures students feel safe and part of a community.

🚍 Transportation: Transportation is available for day scholar students, especially in the Management stream (Grades 11–12).

🔬 Laboratories & Library: Fully equipped labs for Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Computer Science support experiential learning. A well‑stocked library with books and digital resources fosters academic research.

🏃‍♂️ Sports & Physical Activities: The campus includes a sports complex with football, volleyball, tennis, and a gymnasium for regular physical training.

🏥 Medical Support: Two dedicated medical halls with staff ensure 24/7 care; a doctor visits daily for routine check‑ups and health support.

🍃 Environment: Located in a pollution‑free, green hillside area, the campus provides a peaceful atmosphere that promotes focus and wellness.

📅 Student Activities & Achievements

NPS doesn’t just focus on classroom learning — students participate in sports, cultural events, and community programs.

The school has hosted events like the IGP Running Trophy Football Championship, where students showcase teamwork and athletic skills. Students also engage in cultural performances, science exhibitions, and leadership opportunities that support holistic development.
