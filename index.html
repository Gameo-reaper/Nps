🏫 About Nepal Police School (NPS)

Nepal Police School (often called Police School or NPS) is a prestigious co-educational residential school established in 2040 B.S. (1983 CE) under the welfare program of the Nepal Police Organization.

Located about 21 km east of Kathmandu on a hillside campus of over 200 acres of forests and orchards, NPS offers a disciplined, healthy learning environment.

The school’s motto is: “Better Schooling for Ideal Citizens.”

🎯 Vision & Mission

Vision: To transform Nepal Police School into an ideal learning center.

Mission: To foster excellence, creativity, compassion and leadership.

📜 Principal’s Message

“Nepal Police School is committed to quality education that nurtures holistic development.”

🎓 Admissions Information

Admissions are open for Classes IV–VIII and XI.

📚 Facilities & Campus Life

Labs, sports complex, library, hostel and medical facilities are available.

📅 Student Activities & Achievements

Students participate in sports events, science exhibitions and cultural programs.
