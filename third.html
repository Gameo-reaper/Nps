Our Campus & Students

🏛 History & Legacy

Nepal Police School was initially established to educate the children of police personnel. Over the years, it opened its doors to civilian students and became one of Nepal's most respected co-educational residential schools.

The campus spans over 200 acres, with orchards, gardens, and forests — providing a natural, serene environment for learning and development.

📖 Academic Streams & Curriculum

NPS follows the NEB curriculum and prepares students for SEE and SLC examinations. Classes IV to XII are offered with the following streams:

  • Science Stream (Grades XI–XII)
  • Management Stream (Grades XI–XII)
  • Focus on English, Nepali, Mathematics, and Science for lower grades

🎨 Extra-curricular & Co-curricular Programs

Students are encouraged to participate in diverse activities such as:

  • Science Exhibitions 🔬
  • Debates & Quiz Competitions 🏆
  • Music, Dance, and Cultural Programs 🎭
  • Sports Competitions: Football, Volleyball, Athletics ⚽🏐
  • Community Service and Social Awareness Programs 🌱

🏅 Awards & Achievements

NPS students have consistently excelled in academics, sports, and cultural programs at local and national levels. The school has also hosted the IGP Running Trophy Football Championship, highlighting leadership and teamwork.

🎓 Alumni & Community

Graduates of NPS have gone on to pursue higher education across Nepal and abroad. Alumni are active in public service, the corporate sector, and research, reflecting the values of discipline, knowledge, and integrity taught at the school.
